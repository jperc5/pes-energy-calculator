# PES Energy Calculator

A free, open-source calculator that converts **power and time into energy**.

This tool is part of the PES (Percell Energy Services) project and is intended for learning, engineering intuition, and practical estimation.

---

## What it does
- Converts **Power × Time → Energy**
- Supports:
  - Power: W, kW
  - Time: hours, minutes
- Outputs:
  - Wh
  - kWh

---

## Formula
Energy is calculated using:

Energy = Power × Time

Where:
- Power is in watts (W)
- Time is in hours (h)
- Energy is returned in watt-hours (Wh) and kilowatt-hours (kWh)
